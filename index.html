Welcome to math game!

Rules:

The game starts with a player rolling the dice. If the player rolls and receives a 2-6, they add that to their point total. That player will continue to go until they choose to "hold", or they roll a 1. If the player chooses to "hold", then they keep the points they have accumulated for that turn, and add it to their point total. If that player rolls a 1 then they lose all points for that turn and lose their turn. The first player to 100 wins!
